How to Use:

  • Connect Wallet: Click to connect your MetaMask wallet
  • Create Campaign: Set goal in ETH and duration in seconds
  • Pledge: Support campaigns by sending ETH
  • Claim: Campaign creators can claim funds if goal is met
  • Refund: Get your ETH back if campaign fails

Blockchain Features:

  • Decentralized crowdfunding on Ethereum
  • Smart contract automation
  • Transparent and secure transactions
  • No intermediaries required
